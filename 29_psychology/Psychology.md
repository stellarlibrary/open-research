# 29. Psychology

## Degree Programs

| Level | Programs |
|---|---|
| Undergraduate | Psychology |
| Masters | MA/MS in Psychology, I-O Psychology, Counseling |
| Doctoral | PhD, PsyD, specialist subfields |

**Note:** Psychology spans experimental science, quantitative methods, and clinical practice. The undergraduate major is broad; graduate training separates sharply into research and practitioner tracks.

---

## 29.1 Core Curriculum

| Course | Main topics | Standard text |
|---|---|---|
| Intro psychology | Survey of cognition, development, social, abnormal, biological, methods | Myers & DeWall - *Psychology* |
| Research methods | Design, validity, experiments, ethics, APA style | Morling - *Research Methods in Psychology* |
| Statistics | t tests, ANOVA, regression, power, effect sizes | Howell - *Statistical Methods for Psychology* |
| Cognitive psychology | Attention, memory, language, reasoning | Goldstein - *Cognitive Psychology* |
| Social psychology | Attitudes, identity, groups, prejudice, persuasion | Aronson, Wilson & Sommers - *Social Psychology* |
| Developmental psychology | Attachment, cognitive and social development, lifespan | Berk - *Development Through the Lifespan* |
| Abnormal psychology | Psychopathology, diagnosis, etiology, treatment | Kring, Johnson, Davison & Neale - *Abnormal Psychology* |
| Biological psychology | Brain-behavior links, neurotransmitters, imaging, hormones | Breedlove & Watson - *Behavioral Neuroscience* |
| Personality | Trait, psychodynamic, humanistic, social-cognitive theories | Cervone & Pervin - *Personality* |

### Graduate depth
- Clinical and counseling psychology
- Cognitive psychology and cognitive neuroscience
- Social, developmental, personality, and quantitative psychology
- I-O, health, forensic, and behavioral neuroscience tracks

### Recommended papers and landmark studies

#### Foundations and classic theory
- James W. 1890. *The Principles of Psychology*.
- Freud S. major metapsychological and clinical essays.
- Pavlov IP. conditioned reflex studies.
- Watson JB. 1913. behaviorist manifesto. *Psychological Review*.
- Skinner BF. operant conditioning papers.
- Bandura A. 1977. self-efficacy. *Psychological Review*.

#### Learning, cognition, and memory
- Miller GA. 1956. Magical number seven. *Psychological Review*.
- Broadbent DE. 1958. *Perception and Communication*.
- Atkinson RC, Shiffrin RM. 1968. memory model chapter.
- Baddeley AD, Hitch G. 1974. working memory. *Psychology of Learning and Motivation*.
- Tulving E. episodic and semantic memory papers.
- Tversky A, Kahneman D. 1974. heuristics and biases. *Science*.

#### Social psychology
- Festinger L. 1957. *A Theory of Cognitive Dissonance*.
- Asch SE. conformity studies.
- Milgram S. obedience studies. *J Abnormal and Social Psychology*.
- Darley JM, Latané B. bystander intervention papers.
- Tajfel H, Turner JC. social identity theory writings.
- Petty RE, Cacioppo JT. elaboration likelihood model papers.

#### Developmental psychology
- Piaget J. stage theory works.
- Vygotsky LS. *Mind in Society*.
- Bowlby J. attachment writings.
- Ainsworth MDS et al. strange situation studies.
- Baillargeon R. infant cognition violation-of-expectation papers.
- Moffitt TE. developmental psychopathology and life-course studies.

#### Personality, measurement, and quantitative psychology
- Costa PT, McCrae RR. Big Five and NEO papers.
- Cronbach LJ. 1951. coefficient alpha. *Psychometrika*.
- Spearman C. general intelligence papers.
- Beck AT et al. cognitive triad and depression scales.
- Baron RM, Kenny DA. 1986. mediation/moderation framework. *JPSP*.
- Cohen J. 1988. *Statistical Power Analysis for the Behavioral Sciences*.

#### Clinical psychology and psychopathology
- Beck AT. 1967. *Depression: Clinical, Experimental, and Theoretical Aspects*.
- Linehan MM. DBT treatment-development studies.
- Barlow DH. transdiagnostic and anxiety treatment papers.
- Clark DM, Wells A. cognitive model of social phobia.
- Insel T et al. RDoC framework papers.
- Cuijpers P et al. psychotherapy meta-analyses and comparative-effectiveness reviews.

#### Replication, open science, and new directions
- Rosenthal R. 1979. file drawer problem. *Psychological Bulletin*.
- Simmons JP, Nelson LD, Simonsohn U. 2011. false-positive psychology. *Psychological Science*.
- Open Science Collaboration. 2015. reproducibility project. *Science*.
- Nosek BA et al. 2018. preregistration and open-science reform. *PNAS*.
- Henrich J, Heine SJ, Norenzayan A. 2010. WEIRD samples. *Behavioral and Brain Sciences*.
- Muthukrishna M et al. 2020. Beyond WEIRD psychology. *Behavioral and Brain Sciences*.

## 29.4 Open Problems & Frontiers

- Replication, measurement quality, and theory building
- Precision mental health and treatment matching
- Digital phenotyping and privacy
- Cross-cultural generalizability beyond WEIRD samples
- Better integration of biological, cognitive, and social explanation
- Effects of AI and platform life on attention, emotion, and identity
