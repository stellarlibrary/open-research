## 48.5 Open Problems & Research Frontiers

- **AI and managerial work** - Decision support, monitoring, coordination, and algorithmic authority.
- **Hybrid work and organization design** - Culture, innovation, monitoring, and inequality under flexible work arrangements.
- **Leadership and legitimacy** - Trust, stakeholder governance, and institutional fragility.
- **Entrepreneurial ecosystems** - Regional clusters, venture networks, and platform dependence.
- **DEI and organizational justice** - Structural interventions beyond symbolic policy.
- **Sustainability and stakeholder governance** - Measuring purpose, social impact, and long-run performance.
