---

## 46.2 Graduate Specialization Areas

#### A. Corporate Finance
- Investment policy, payout, governance, venture capital, private equity, innovation finance.

#### B. Asset Pricing
- Factors, anomalies, risk premia, market microstructure, limits to arbitrage.

#### C. Banking & Intermediation
- Regulation, liquidity, systemic risk, credit supply, shadow banking.

#### D. Quantitative Finance
- Stochastic calculus, computational pricing, Monte Carlo, risk management.

#### E. Behavioral and Household Finance
- Biases, investor attention, financial literacy, household balance sheets.

## 46.5 Open Problems & Research Frontiers

- **AI in markets** - Model risk, crowding, autonomous trading, and explainability.
- **Climate finance** - Pricing transition and physical risk, carbon markets, disclosure quality.
- **Fintech and payments** - Stablecoins, tokenization, embedded finance, and regulatory design.
- **Systemic resilience** - Intermediation outside banks and cross-market contagion.
- **Household balance sheets** - Debt, housing, inequality, and financial fragility.
- **Market structure** - Fragmentation, high-frequency trading, and retail market design.
