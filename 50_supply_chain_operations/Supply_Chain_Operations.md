## 50.1 Core Curriculum

### Core Courses

| Course | Content |
|---|---|
| **Operations Management** | Process analysis, capacity, quality, lean, project and service operations |
| **Supply Chain Management** | Network design, sourcing, coordination, contracts, risk, sustainability |
| **Logistics & Transportation** | Modes, warehousing, fulfillment, last-mile, reverse logistics |
| **Inventory Management** | EOQ, safety stock, multi-echelon systems, bullwhip dynamics |
| **Procurement & Sourcing** | Supplier selection, contracts, spend analysis, negotiation |
| **Forecasting & S&OP** | Demand planning, statistical forecasting, planning integration |
| **Supply Chain Analytics** | Optimization, simulation, dashboards, digital twins |

## 50.2 Graduate Specialization Areas

#### A. Operations Strategy
- Process choice, capacity, quality, service operations, behavioral operations.

#### B. Supply Chain Coordination
- Contracts, information sharing, incentives, resilience, disruption management.

#### C. Logistics & Transportation
- Routing, network design, e-commerce fulfillment, intermodal systems.

#### D. Sustainable & Humanitarian Supply Chains
- Carbon accounting, circularity, sourcing ethics, disaster logistics.

#### E. Analytics / OR-adjacent Research
- Stochastic models, optimization, forecasting, simulation, revenue management.

## 50.5 Open Problems & Research Frontiers

- **Resilience and geopolitics** - Nearshoring, redundancy, and network fragility.
- **AI and autonomous planning** - Forecasting, procurement, robotics, and decision support.
- **Sustainability and Scope 3** - Measuring emissions, circularity, and supplier accountability.
- **E-commerce fulfillment** - Returns, micro-fulfillment, and service-level economics.
- **Visibility and data sharing** - Multi-tier mapping, interoperability, and trust.
- **Behavioral operations** - Human judgment, incentives, and implementation gaps.
