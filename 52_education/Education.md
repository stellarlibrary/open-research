## 52.1 Core Curriculum

### Teacher Preparation (Undergraduate/MAT)

| Course | Content |
|---|---|
| **Educational Psychology** | Learning, development, motivation, assessment, classroom management |
| **Foundations of Education** | History, philosophy, sociology, policy, inequality |
| **Curriculum & Instruction** | Backward design, standards, lesson planning, pedagogy |
| **Assessment** | Formative and summative assessment, validity, rubrics, data use |
| **Methods Courses** | Subject-specific pedagogy, literacy, STEM, social studies, arts |
| **Inclusive / Special Education** | UDL, RTI, IEPs, disability studies, differentiation |
| **Educational Technology** | LMS, digital pedagogy, online learning, AI literacy |
| **Student Teaching / Practicum** | Supervised classroom practice and reflective coaching |

### Graduate Research Programs (PhD/EdD)

| Focus Area | Topics |
|---|---|
| **Curriculum & Teaching** | Pedagogical content knowledge, teacher learning, classroom inquiry |
| **Policy & Leadership** | Governance, finance, accountability, organizational change |
| **Learning Sciences** | Cognition, design-based research, collaborative learning, analytics |
| **Higher Education** | Access, persistence, student development, institutions |
| **Special Education** | Intervention, inclusion, transition, disability and schooling |
| **Measurement / Psychometrics** | Test theory, IRT, evaluation, accountability |

## 52.4 Open Problems & Research Frontiers

- **AI in teaching and assessment** - Tutor systems, integrity, feedback, and teacher workload.
- **Teacher recruitment and retention** - Shortages, burnout, compensation, and professional status.
- **Learning recovery and wellbeing** - Attendance, mental health, tutoring, and intervention design.
- **Science of reading and curriculum wars** - Translating evidence into systems and teacher prep.
- **Equity and school finance** - Persistent stratification by race, language, disability, and geography.
- **Higher education change** - Cost, enrollment, alternative credentials, and digital delivery.
