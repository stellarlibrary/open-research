# 56. Neuroscience

## Degree Programs

| Level | Programs |
|---|---|
| Undergraduate | Neuroscience, Behavioral Neuroscience, Cognitive Neuroscience |
| Masters | Neuroimaging, Clinical Neuroscience, Computational Neuroscience |
| PhD | Neuroscience, Cellular and Molecular Neuroscience, Systems, Cognitive, Computational, Translational Neuroscience |

**Note:** Neuroscience integrates molecular biology, physiology, psychology, computation, and clinical disease research. It is one of the clearest examples of an interdisciplinary major that still has a strong common canon.

## 56.1 Core Curriculum

| Course | Main topics | Standard text |

### Recommended papers and landmark studies

#### Foundations of the neuron doctrine and electrophysiology
- Ramón y Cajal S. foundational neuron doctrine papers and histological work.
- Sherrington CS. 1906. *The Integrative Action of the Nervous System*.
- Hodgkin AL, Huxley AF. 1952. Action potential model papers. *J Physiology*.
- Eccles JC. synaptic physiology studies.
- Katz B. quantal transmitter release papers.
- Hubel DH, Wiesel TN. receptive field and cortical column papers. *J Physiology*.

#### Synapses, plasticity, and memory
- Hebb DO. 1949. *The Organization of Behavior*.
- Bliss TVP, Lømo T. 1973. Long-term potentiation. *J Physiology*.
- Kandel ER et al. Aplysia learning and synaptic plasticity papers.
- Malenka RC, Bear MF. 2004. LTP/LTD review. *Neuron*.
- Turrigiano GG. 1999 and later. Homeostatic plasticity papers. *Cell* / *Neuron*.
- Josselyn SA, Tonegawa S. engram review papers. *Science*.

#### Systems, coding, and behavior
- O'Keefe J, Dostrovsky J. 1971. Place cells. *Brain Research*.
- Hafting T et al. 2005. Grid cells. *Nature*.
- Georgopoulos AP et al. population vector coding. *Science*.
- Shadlen MN, Newsome WT. decision-related firing and accumulation papers.
- Churchland MM et al. neural population dynamics in motor cortex. *Nature*.
- Marder E, Goaillard JM. circuit robustness and degeneracy reviews. *Nature Reviews Neuroscience*.

#### Cognitive neuroscience and human neuroimaging
- Broca P. 1861. speech localization papers.
- Wernicke C. 1874. language comprehension localization work.
- Milner B. H.M. memory studies.
- Scoville WB, Milner B. 1957. hippocampus and memory. *J Neurol Neurosurg Psychiatry*.
- Raichle ME et al. 2001. Default mode network. *PNAS*.
- Kanwisher N et al. fusiform face area papers. *J Neuroscience*.

#### Development, cell types, and modern methods
- Rakic P. neuronal migration and cortical development papers.
- Luo L, Callaway EM, Svoboda K. 2018. genetic dissection of circuits. *Neuron*.
- Deisseroth K. 2011. optogenetics overview. *Nature Methods*.
- Boyden ES et al. 2005. millisecond-timescale optical control. *Nat Neurosci*.
- Stuart T, Satija R. single-cell analysis method papers.
- Yuste R et al. BRAIN Initiative vision papers. *Neuron*.

#### Disease, translation, and neurotechnology
- Hardy J, Higgins G. 1992. amyloid cascade hypothesis. *Science*.
- Braak H, Braak E. staging papers in Alzheimer's and Parkinson's pathology.
- Mayberg HS et al. depression circuit and DBS papers.
- Perlmutter JS and Benabid AL key DBS studies in movement disorders.
- Pandarinath C et al. speech and motor BCI decoding papers.
- Nature and Science reviews on psychedelics, neuroimmune disease, and cell atlases now serve as standard frontier readings.

## 56.4 Open Problems & Frontiers

- Neural coding across scales from synapse to whole brain
- Mechanisms of memory storage and reconsolidation
- Circuit-level explanations of psychiatric disease
- Connectomics linked to function rather than anatomy alone
- Safer, more useful BCIs and neuromodulation
- Integration of AI models with biological interpretability
