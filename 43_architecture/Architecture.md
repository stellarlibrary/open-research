# 43. Architecture

## Degree Programs

| Level | Programs |
|---|---|
| Undergraduate | BArch, pre-professional BS/BA in Architecture, Architectural Studies |
| Masters | MArch, MS in Architecture, Urban Design, Building Technology |
| PhD | Architectural History/Theory, Design Research, Building Science |
| Professional | Licensure via accredited degree, AXP, and ARE |

---

## 43.1 Core Curriculum

### Core Architecture Courses

| Course | Content |
|---|---|
| **Design Studio** | Sequential studios from form and space to building and urban-scale problems |
| **Architectural History** | Ancient to contemporary global architectural history |
| **Structures** | Statics, systems, material behavior, building loads |
| **Environmental Systems** | Climate, daylight, HVAC, acoustics, energy modeling |
| **Construction / Materials** | Assemblies, details, codes, envelopes, tectonics |
| **Digital Representation** | CAD, BIM, modeling, rendering, diagrams, fabrication workflows |
| **Theory** | Vitruvius to contemporary critical, phenomenological, ecological, and political theory |
| **Professional Practice** | Contracts, delivery methods, ethics, regulation, office practice |

---

## 43.2 Graduate Specialization Areas

#### A. Advanced Design
- Housing, institutions, adaptive reuse, thesis studios, urban interventions.

#### B. Urban Design & Planning Interface
- Public space, zoning, infrastructure, transit-oriented development, regional form.

#### C. Building Technology / Sustainability
- Building science, envelopes, fabrication, carbon accounting, mass timber, simulation.

#### D. History, Theory & Criticism
- Historiography, preservation, coloniality, race/gender/space, exhibition history.

#### E. Computational Design
- Parametrics, generative systems, robotics, AI-assisted design, digital twins.

## 43.3 Key Methods & Tools

| Method | Use |
|---|---|
| **Studio Critique** | Iterative design development and verbal/visual defense |
| **Drawing & Modeling** | Plan, section, elevation, diagram, physical and digital model |
| **Building Analysis** | Precedent study, program analysis, post-occupancy evaluation |
| **Environmental Simulation** | Daylight, thermal, airflow, energy, carbon performance |
| **Fabrication / Prototyping** | Mockups, CNC, laser cutting, robotic workflows |
| **Historical / Theoretical Research** | Archives, treatises, case studies, visual analysis |

## 43.5 Open Problems & Research Frontiers

- **Decarbonization** - Embodied carbon, adaptive reuse, and operational performance.
- **Housing and spatial justice** - Affordability, informal urbanism, displacement, and public space.
- **AI and computation** - Generative design, automation, liability, and authorship in practice.
- **Resilience** - Fire, flood, heat, and retreat/fortification strategies.
- **Preservation and repair** - Heritage, reuse, maintenance, and colonial memory.
- **Professional transformation** - Labor conditions, licensure, procurement, and multidisciplinary practice.
