# 34. Communications & Media Studies

## Degree Programs

| Level | Programs |
|---|---|
| Undergraduate | Communication, Media Studies, Journalism, PR, Advertising |
| Masters | Communication, Strategic Communication, Media Studies, Journalism |
| PhD | Communication, Mass Communication, Media Studies, Rhetoric |

**Note:** Communication studies mixes humanistic and social-scientific traditions. Programs usually cover interpersonal communication, media effects, journalism, rhetoric, organizational communication, and digital media.

---

## 34.1 Core Curriculum

| Course | Main topics | Standard text |
|---|---|---|
| Intro communication | Models, channels, contexts, interpersonal and mass communication | Griffin, Ledbetter & Sparks - *A First Look at Communication Theory* |
| Mass communication and society | Media institutions, effects, public sphere, platforms | Baran & Davis - *Mass Communication Theory* |
| Interpersonal communication | Relational development, self-disclosure, conflict, listening | Beebe, Beebe & Redmond or Baxter/Braithwaite frameworks |
| Organizational communication | Leadership, culture, networks, crisis, internal communication | Miller - *Organizational Communication* |
| Rhetoric | Persuasion, argument, public address, criticism | Foss - *Rhetorical Criticism* |
| Media research methods | Surveys, experiments, content analysis, qualitative methods | Wimmer & Dominick - *Mass Media Research* |
| Journalism | Reporting, verification, ethics, digital production | Kovach & Rosenstiel - *The Elements of Journalism* |
| PR/strategic communication | Campaigns, audience analysis, crisis communication | Wilcox & Cameron or equivalent core texts |

### Graduate depth
- Media effects and political communication
- Digital media and platform studies
- Health communication and risk communication
- Organizational and interpersonal communication
- Critical/cultural studies and rhetoric
- Journalism studies and news ecosystems

### Recommended papers and landmark studies

#### Foundational theories of communication and media
- Shannon CE, Weaver W. 1949. *The Mathematical Theory of Communication*.
- Lasswell HD. 1948. communication model essay.
- Lazarsfeld PF, Berelson B, Gaudet H. 1944. *The People's Choice*.
- Katz E, Lazarsfeld PF. 1955. *Personal Influence*.
- McCombs ME, Shaw DL. 1972. agenda-setting. *Public Opinion Quarterly*.
- Gerbner G, Gross L. cultivation theory papers.

#### Interpersonal and relational communication
- Altman I, Taylor D. 1973. *Social Penetration*.
- Knapp ML. relational development frameworks.
- Baxter LA, Montgomery BM. relational dialectics theory writings.
- Watzlawick P, Beavin J, Jackson D. *Pragmatics of Human Communication*.
- Burgoon JK. expectancy violations theory papers.
- Gottman JM. relationship communication and conflict studies.

#### Rhetoric, public sphere, and critical theory
- Aristotle. *Rhetoric*.
- Burke K. dramatism and identification writings.
- Habermas J. *The Structural Transformation of the Public Sphere*.
- Hall S. encoding/decoding. *Culture, Media, Language*.
- Foucault M. discourse and power writings as major critical references.
- McLuhan M. *Understanding Media*.

#### Journalism, gatekeeping, and news production
- White DM. 1950. gatekeeper study. *Journalism Quarterly*.
- Gans H. 1979. *Deciding What's News*.
- Tuchman G. 1978. *Making News*.
- Entman RM. 1993. framing paradigm. *Journal of Communication*.
- Kovach B, Rosenstiel T. verification and journalism ethics framework.
- Bennett WL. indexing hypothesis and news-politics studies.

#### Media effects, politics, and misinformation
- Iyengar S, Kinder DR. 1987. *News That Matters*.
- Zaller J. 1992. *The Nature and Origins of Mass Opinion*.
- Jamieson KH, Cappella JN. 2008. *Echo Chamber*.
- Guess A, Nyhan B, Reifler J. fake-news exposure studies.
- Vosoughi S, Roy D, Aral S. 2018. spread of true and false news. *Science*.
- Pennycook G, Rand DG. misinformation and accuracy-nudge studies.

#### Digital media, platforms, and contemporary communication
- boyd d. 2014. *It's Complicated*.
- Papacharissi Z. networked publics and affective publics writings.
- Gillespie T. 2018. *Custodians of the Internet*.
- Nieborg DB, Poell T. platformization of cultural production papers.
- Couldry N, Mejias UA. data colonialism framework.
- Napoli PM. algorithmic gatekeeping and platform governance studies.

#### Health and crisis communication
- Rogers EM. 1962. *Diffusion of Innovations*.
- Witte K. extended parallel process model papers.
- Kreps GL. health communication framework writings.
- Reynolds B, Seeger MW. crisis and emergency risk communication. *Journal of Health Communication*.
- Southwell BG et al. infodemic and misinformation studies.
- Hornik RC. public health campaign evaluation literature.

## 34.4 Open Problems & Frontiers

- Misinformation, synthetic media, and trust repair
- Platform governance and algorithmic transparency
- Sustainable futures for journalism and local news
- Better methods for combining critical and computational media analysis
- Health communication under polarization and low trust
- Labor, authenticity, and governance in the creator economy
