## 49.2 Graduate Specialization Areas (PhD)

#### A. Business Value of IT
- IT investment, productivity, complementarities, digital transformation.

#### B. Technology Adoption and Use
- TAM, UTAUT, post-adoption behavior, resistance, digital divide.

#### C. Platforms, Ecosystems, and Innovation
- APIs, marketplaces, modularity, ecosystem governance, digital entrepreneurship.

#### D. Security, Privacy, and Governance
- Risk, compliance, privacy behavior, cybersecurity management.

#### E. Sectoral IS Research
- Healthcare IT, public-sector systems, fintech, social media, education technology.

## 49.5 Open Problems & Research Frontiers

- **Enterprise AI governance** - Deployment, trust, accountability, and organizational redesign.
- **Digital transformation measurement** - Distinguishing genuine capability change from IT spending alone.
- **Platform dependence** - Vendor lock-in, API governance, and ecosystem power.
- **Data governance** - Quality, lineage, privacy, sovereignty, and compliance across clouds.
- **Cybersecurity as organizational behavior** - Human factors, incentive design, and resilience.
- **Low-code and automation** - Citizen development, control, and the future of IS work.
