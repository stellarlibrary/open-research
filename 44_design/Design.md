# 44. Design

## Degree Programs

| Level | Programs |
|---|---|
| Undergraduate | Graphic Design, Industrial Design, Interaction/UX Design, Fashion Design, Interior Design |
| Masters | MFA in Graphic or Industrial Design, MDes, MS in HCI/UX, MA in Design Research |
| PhD | Design Studies, Design Research, Human-Centered Design |

---

## 44.1 Core Curriculum

### Shared Design Foundations

| Course | Content |
|---|---|
| **Design Fundamentals** | Form, composition, hierarchy, critique, iteration |
| **Typography** | Type anatomy, hierarchy, grid, editorial systems |
| **Color & Perception** | Color interaction, contrast, meaning, accessibility |
| **Design History** | Arts and Crafts, Bauhaus, Swiss style, postmodernism, global design histories |
| **Design Process / Research** | Human-centered, participatory, service, and systems design methods |
| **Digital Tools** | Adobe suite, Figma, CAD, prototyping, 3D tools |

### Graphic / Visual Communication Design
- Identity systems, editorial design, packaging, motion, web, campaign work.

### Industrial / Product Design
- Sketching, ergonomics, materials, prototyping, CAD/CAM, manufacturing.

### Interaction / UX Design
- User research, IA, flows, wireframing, usability, service design, accessibility.

## 44.2 Key Methods & Tools

| Method | Application |
|---|---|
| **Studio Critique** | Feedback-led iteration and rationale building |
| **Prototyping** | Paper, digital, physical, and service prototypes |
| **User Research** | Interviews, observation, usability testing, co-design |
| **Systems Thinking** | Mapping stakeholders, journeys, infrastructures, and services |
| **Visual Systems** | Grid, type, identity, information design |
| **Fabrication / CAD** | Product development, rendering, modelmaking |

## 44.4 Open Problems & Research Frontiers

- **Generative AI and creative labor** - Shifting workflows, authorship, and skill formation.
- **Accessibility and inclusive design** - Beyond compliance toward disability-led design practice.
- **Sustainability and circularity** - Materials, repairability, lifecycle design, and service substitution.
- **Design justice** - Power, extraction, community governance, and participatory methods.
- **Platform interfaces and dark patterns** - Ethics, regulation, and persuasive architectures.
- **Post-screen interaction** - Voice, spatial computing, ambient systems, and embodied interfaces.
