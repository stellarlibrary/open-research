## 45.1 Core Curriculum

### Core Courses

| Course | Content |
|---|---|
| **Financial Accounting** | Double-entry, statements, accruals, revenue, receivables, inventories |
| **Managerial / Cost Accounting** | Cost behavior, budgeting, variance, responsibility centers, decision analysis |
| **Intermediate Accounting** | Advanced GAAP/IFRS topics, leases, pensions, deferred taxes, EPS |

## 45.4 Open Problems & Research Frontiers

- **ESG and assurance** - Standardization, materiality, and auditability of sustainability disclosures.
- **AI and automation** - Judgment, control, and liability in increasingly automated accounting work.
- **Intangibles and digital firms** - Limits of balance-sheet recognition and valuation.
- **Fraud analytics** - Continuous auditing and anomaly detection at scale.
- **Global tax coordination** - Minimum tax, transfer pricing, and digital economy enforcement.
- **Crypto and tokenized assets** - Recognition, controls, and assurance for novel asset classes.
