# 53. Agricultural Sciences

## Degree Programs

| Level | Programs |
|---|---|
| Undergraduate | Agricultural Science, Agronomy, Animal Science, Food Science, Horticulture, Soil Science |

---

## 53.1 Core Curriculum

### Major Sub-disciplines

| Area | Content |
|---|---|
| **Agronomy / Crop Science** | Crop physiology, breeding, genetics, production systems, weed science |
| **Animal Science** | Nutrition, physiology, genetics, reproduction, welfare, production systems |
| **Soil Science** | Soil chemistry, physics, biology, fertility, classification, conservation |
| **Plant Pathology / Entomology** | Disease, pests, epidemiology, IPM, host-pathogen systems |
| **Food Science** | Chemistry, microbiology, processing, safety, sensory science |
| **Agricultural Economics / Systems** | Farm management, commodity markets, policy, technology adoption |
| **Horticulture** | Fruit, vegetable, ornamental production, greenhouse and postharvest systems |

## 53.2 Key Methods & Tools

| Method | Application |
|---|---|
| **Field Trials** | Variety testing, fertilizer response, cropping systems comparison |
| **Breeding / Genetics** | Selection, molecular markers, genomic prediction, gene editing |
| **Laboratory Analysis** | Soil nutrients, feed quality, pathogens, food chemistry |

## 53.4 Open Problems & Research Frontiers

- **Climate-resilient production** - Drought, heat, salinity, and water stress adaptation.
- **Gene editing and regulation** - CRISPR adoption, biosafety, and equitable access.
- **Soil health** - Carbon, microbiomes, nutrient cycling, and measurement standards.
- **Sustainable intensification** - Raising yields while reducing ecological damage.
- **Alternative proteins and food systems** - Cellular agriculture, plant proteins, feed innovation.
- **One Health in agriculture** - Antimicrobial resistance, zoonoses, and food safety.
